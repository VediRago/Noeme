```text
    NOEME
  THE ALIEN
  written by
Diogo Oliveira


EXT. ERIT IMPERIAL GARDENS — DAY

Two siblings play under the shadow of the emperor's statue.

The gardens around it are pristine green, sealed off by
golden fences and watched from every side.

ISILA and CARES drink from the nearby fountain before heading
home.

EXT. DIRT ROAD — INSULAE PATH — CONTINUOUS

The insulae connect through a dirt road hidden from the
public path.

ISILA and CARES walk it kicking rocks, shoving each other,
and laughing too loudly for a place that wants silence.

Then a beam of light cuts across the road.

A child appears in front of them.

Both siblings step back.

CARES stays closest.

ISILA looks around, then rushes to the nearest tree and
breaks off a stick.

CARES reaches out and touches the girl's shoulder.

                         CARES
               I think it's safe.

ISILA nearly slips against the wall as she hurries back.

                         ISILA
               What is it?

CARES shrugs.

                         CARES
               I'd say it's a girl.

                         ISILA
               Of course it's a girl.

ISILA steps closer.

                           ISILA (CONT'D)


                  But where did she come from?
                  The sky?

They both look up.

Then back down.

The girl does not move.

                            ISILA (CONT'D)
                  Are you all right?

ISILA touches the girl's shoulder.

The girl looks directly at her.

ISILA jumps back.

CARES looks up again.

                            CARES
                  Do you think she can fly?

                            ISILA
                  I don't know.

ISILA leans closer.

                            ISILA (CONT'D)
                  But look at what she's wearing.

CARES circles the girl.

                            CARES
                  Is that a bag?

ISILA takes the backpack before the question finishes.

ISILA gives herself no time to feel guilty before opening
it.

                            CARES (CONT'D)
                  She has tiny hidden pockets in
                  her clothes!

CARES starts checking them.

The girl only stands there, looking from one child to the
other without saying a word.


                         ISILA
               She's an alien, Cares.

ISILA spreads crayons and two sandwiches across the dirt.

                           ISILA (CONT'D)
               Look.

CARES crouches over them.

                         CARES
               Maybe she fell out of a passing
               car.

                         ISILA
               And landed here?

                         CARES
               Maybe that's why Father always
               tells us to lock the doors.

                           ISILA
               Must be.

CARES pulls out a folded piece of paper.

                         CARES (CONT'D)
               Hey. Look here.

An old man slows on the path.

                         OLD MAN
               What are you two doing to that
               poor little girl?

                         ISILA
               We think she's an alien.

ISILA says it without hesitation.

                         ISILA (CONT'D)
               She fell from the sky right
               here.

The old man looks at the girl.

Then at the scattered crayons.

                           OLD MAN


               You kids...

He keeps walking.

                         ISILA
                    (whispering)
               Cares, take the paper to the
               scriptorium machine. Maybe
               there's a clue.

CARES grabs the girl's arm and pulls her along.

                         CARES
               Come on, little one. We won't
               let you get lost. We'll find
               your father.

The girl follows.

She does not cry.

INT. PUBLIC SCRIPTORIUM — CONTINUOUS

ISILA smooths the paper against the scanner while CARES
starts pressing buttons.

The machine clicks.

A voice answers.

                         MACHINE VOICE
               Error. Unknown.

ISILA opens her mouth.

Then leans close to CARES.

                         ISILA
               She's an alien.

CARES presses another button.

                         MACHINE VOICE
               Error. Unknown.

Another.

                         MACHINE VOICE
               Error. Unknown.


They keep going.

Citizens pass by without giving much attention to the two
children beating a public machine while a silent girl
stands beside them.

Then the machine stutters.

The voice changes.

                         MACHINE VOICE (CONT'D)
               Please take care of my
               daughter. Her name is Sera, and
               she is from another world.

Both siblings jump back.

They stare at her.

CARES points.

                         CARES
               You are an alien.

A passerby stops for a second.

Then he shrugs and continues on his way.

                         ISILA
               We need to tell Mom.

                         CARES
               Put her on my back.

CARES kneels.

                         CARES (CONT'D)
               Let's run as fast as we can.

CARES takes off with SERA clinging to him.

ISILA stays behind for a few steps, looking around to see if
anyone noticed.

Then ISILA dashes after them.

EXT. STREET — INSULAE PATH — CONTINUOUS

They are still far from the door when both siblings start


shouting.

                          CARES & ISILA
               MOOOOM!

                          ELENA (O.S.)
               What?

ELENA appears barefoot, one hand over her eyes against the
light.

                          CARES & ISILA
               MOM!

ELENA starts running toward them.

                         ELENA
               What's wrong? What happened?

The children stop in front of her, both trying to catch
their breath.

ELENA looks past them.

Her voice softens.

                         ELENA (CONT'D)
               Who is this little girl?

CARES points at SERA.

                         CARES
               Mom, you won't believe this—

ISILA cuts him off.

                         ISILA
               She's an alien!

ELENA kneels in front of SERA.

For a moment, nothing is said.

ELENA extends her hand.

                         ELENA
               Come inside, little one.

                                          FADE TO BLACK.


END ACT 07
```
