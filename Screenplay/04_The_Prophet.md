NOEME

THE PROPHET

written by

Diogo Oliveira


INT. PHIL & ABBY'S BEDROOM — NIGHT

Lamplight warms the room. Shutters soften the city sounds outside.

PHIL reads beside ABBY, who leans against pillows with her phone.

The turning of pages.

ABBY

You know what I saw today?

ABBY lowers her phone and looks down at him.

PHIL

(still reading)

What?

ABBY

Sera.

PHIL lowers the page slightly.

ABBY (CONT'D)

She's becoming such a beautiful girl.

PHIL lets the book fall to his chest and turns toward her.

PHIL

Yeah.

A small smile reaches PHIL before he can stop it.

Her eyes are yours. Those green eyes are the only reason I fell for you.

ABBY kicks him under the blanket.

ABBY (CONT'D)

The only reason, huh?

ABBY turns back to her phone.

From your perspective, they weren't your target most of the time.

PHIL

Her hair, though. Do you have anyone in your family with red hair?

ABBY lowers the phone.

ABBY (CONT'D)

Oh. Yeah. About that.

ABBY turns to face him.
