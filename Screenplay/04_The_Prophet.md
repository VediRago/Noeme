```text
    NOEME
 THE PROPHET
  written by
Diogo Oliveira


INT. PHIL & ABBY'S BEDROOM — NIGHT

Lamplight warms the room. Shutters soften the city sounds
outside.

PHIL reads beside ABBY, who leans against pillows with her
phone.

The turning of pages.

                         ABBY
               You know what I saw today?

ABBY lowers her phone and looks down at him.

                            PHIL
                       (still reading)
               What?

                            ABBY
               Sera.

PHIL lowers the page slightly.

                         ABBY (CONT'D)
               She's becoming such a beautiful
               girl.

PHIL lets the book fall to his chest and turns toward her.

                            PHIL
               Yeah.

A small smile reaches PHIL before he can stop it.

               Her eyes are yours. Those green
               eyes are the only reason I fell
               for you.

ABBY kicks him under the blanket.

                         ABBY (CONT'D)
               The only reason, huh?

ABBY turns back to her phone.

               From your perspective, they
               weren't your target most of the
               time.


                         PHIL
               Her hair, though. Do you have
               anyone in your family with red
               hair?

ABBY lowers the phone.

                         ABBY (CONT'D)
               Oh. Yeah. About that.

ABBY turns to face him.

                         ABBY (CONT'D)
               I cheated on you.

PHIL shifts upright.

                          PHIL
               What?

                         ABBY
               You wouldn't shut up about the
               thing. Saving people. It got
               tiring. It's like being with a
               prophet, and I don't want that.

ABBY picks up her phone again.

PHIL stares at her.

                          PHIL
               What?

                         ABBY
                    (over the phone)
               Did it finally work? After all
               these years?

PHIL goes under the blankets.

                          ABBY (CONT'D)
               PHIL!

ABBY knees him away.

                          ABBY (CONT'D)
               Stop.

PHIL does not stop. He tickles her.


                            ABBY (CONT'D)
               STOP!

ABBY grabs the remote and turns on the TV while PHIL
tickles her harder.

                            ABBY (CONT'D)
               STOP!

A broadcast voice enters.

                         BROADCAST VOICE (V.O.)
               ...if you have any of these
               symptoms, please call the
               number below.

ABBY hits the top of PHIL's head with her fist.

                         ABBY
               I told you.

PHIL emerges from under the blankets, one hand on his head.

                         BROADCAST VOICE (V.O.)
               Do not leave your home. If you
               have a cough, weakness,
               difficulty breathing, or signs
               of blood—

The playfulness drains from the room.

                         ABBY
               Even in bed, we're bombarded
               with tragedies.

ABBY reaches for the remote.

                         PHIL
               They're saying at work it may
               become a calamity.

                         ABBY
               Oh, are they?

ABBY looks at him.

                         ABBY (CONT'D)
               What else do they tell the
               almighty savior of humanity?


ABBY changes the channel.

A bright field fills the screen.

A family runs through grass too green to be real.

                         ADVERTISEMENT VOICE (V.O.)
               Ever thought of a new life? How
               about a new start on a lush
               green planet? Start again.
               Build again. Give your children
               a future beyond the walls.

PHIL puts his hand over the remote.

                            PHIL
               Wait.

ABBY follows his eyes to the television.

                         ABBY
               One of yours?

                         PHIL
               They aren't lush.

A small laugh slips from ABBY.

                         ABBY
               From prophet to god in less
               than fifteen minutes.

PHIL does not laugh. His eyes stay on the screen.

The family keeps smiling. Children run beneath a sky
without smoke.

PHIL lowers his voice.

                         PHIL
               Seriously, Abby. We don't even
               know where we send them.

ABBY looks at him.

                            ABBY
               What?

                            PHIL


                We never hear from anyone. No
                one comes back. We think they
                go to other worlds, but to be
                honest, we don't know for
                certain.

ABBY throws the remote at his chest.

                          ABBY
                Are you kidding me?

ABBY gets out of bed and turns to face him.

                          ABBY (CONT'D)
                And you're okay with that?

PHIL sits up.

                          PHIL
                Abby—

                          ABBY
                Are you sending innocent people
                to death?

ABBY's hands land on her waist.

                          PHIL
                I don't know.

PHIL looks straight at her.

                          PHIL (CONT'D)
                What do you want me to do?

The question leaves PHIL with less force than he wanted. He
falls back against the bed and stares at the ceiling.

                          PHIL
                Look at us.

The advertisement keeps shining over the room.

                          PHIL (CONT'D)
                You wanted to leave that damn
                city, right?

PHIL's voice thins.


                         PHIL (CONT'D)
               We're safe.

ABBY says nothing.

The advertisement continues.

The family runs through impossible grass.

                                         FADE TO BLACK.
```
