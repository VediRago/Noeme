# 10 — Sera / Rain Alley

```screenplay
EXT. CITY STREET — RAIN — DAY

Rainwater beneath SERA'S feet detaches her from the flow of bodies around her.

Each brush challenges balance.

The next body pushes her back to center.

Her eyes stay fixed on footsteps rippling through puddles that never meet.

A blink.

One foot hangs mid-motion.

Another blink.

Her head turns toward an alley the crowd had hidden.

Realization begins to settle—

Denied.

Everything snaps back into motion.

Sera looks ahead, uncertain which direction still belongs to her.

So she walks backward.

Defying the will to go forward.

Her body reaches the alley entrance before the choice does.

A passing car splashes her.

Warning or push forward — unclear.

It takes no effort to turn toward the darkness.

She goes.

INT. ALLEY — CONTINUOUS

Shadow shrinks with each step, pulsing under passing headlights.

Her final step strikes a bottle.

The sound skitters through the pavement.

A desperate face emerges from the dark.

Sera's gaze drops.

Blackened feet.

A shivering body.

A hood beginning to hide eyes turning away from her.

Sera tilts her head.

Her hand lands on her waist.

SERA  
You look like this city has given a face.

The hooded figure's eyes snap back.

Narrowing.

SERA  
Oh really?

Sera shifts her head to the other side while reaching into the opposite pocket.

SERA  
There are tons. I mean a TON of loonies around here.

She crouches down to the figure's height and offers a chocolate.

SERA  
But you ain't one of 'em.

Sera smiles.

Voices appear, lingering at the mouth of the alley.

VOICE (O.S.)  
Hey, little girl—

The world goes mute.

A black wall passes by.

White.

Black again.

When Sera's head stops, there are only bodies.

The sound of a wrapper tingles at Sera's ear.

She is still holding her breath.

When she decides to face the figure, she does it very slowly.

Standing on the tips of her toes for no good reason.

SERA  
And soo... what's your name—

Her lips have not even touched to finish the sentence.

Her eyes widen.

SERA  
Kill?!?!

She looks down.

An extended palm.

An empty wrapper.
```

NOTE: Kael does not speak her name aloud. She communicates it telepathically. Sera receives or understands the name as “Kill,” not “Kael.”
