```text
    NOEME
   THE HINT
  written by
Diogo Oliveira


INT. VENISA'S HOME — DINING AREA — NIGHT

ISILA, CARES, and SERA sit at the table while VENISA prepares
dinner.

The siblings play with each other, laughing between bites
of air and unfinished words.

SERA slides her finger across the cracks in the wooden
table.

It stops when a distant door slams shut.

                         LURAS (O.S.)
               Why are you two making so much
               noise?

LURAS shuts the door hard behind him. As he passes SERA,
his shoulder clips her.

                           LURAS
               Sit down!

The siblings go still.

                         LURAS (CONT'D)
               Venisa. Room. Now.

VENISA looks at SERA once, then follows him.

ISILA and CARES slip away toward their room, still whispering
to each other, their laughter smaller now.

SERA lowers her eyes to the table.

Her finger starts moving again.

It does not last long.

VENISA begins crying behind the door.

SERA's head turns.

Heavy footsteps come closer.

Her finger detaches from the crack.

LURAS comes out first.

                           LURAS


                  Let's go. We have to take you
                  to the clinic.

VENISA follows behind him, crying, unable to look directly
at SERA.

                            SERA
                  Why? Is she sick?

No one answers.

SERA stands.

                            SERA (CONT'D)
                  Okay. I'll go.

LURAS grabs his coat.

                            LURAS
                  You didn't really have an
                  option. Let's go.

As SERA leaves, VENISA rushes after LURAS and grabs his arm.

                            VENISA
                  Please. She's just a child.
                  It's been seven years, Luras.
                  She's our own.

LURAS pulls his arm free.

                            LURAS
                  And I'm somehow unaware of that?

He turns back to her.

                            LURAS (CONT'D)
                  Look at us. Look at me.

His mouth opens again, but nothing comes out.

He shakes his head and leaves.

EXT. VENISA'S HOME — NIGHT

SERA waits outside, leaning against the wall, looking at
the sky.

When SERA'S eyes shift toward LURAS, she catches his hand


crossing his face as he walks through the dim entryway.

                          LURAS
                Come on. Let's go.

LURAS passes her and walks ahead.

EXT. CLINIC — NIGHT

The clinic does not take long to come into view.

Two LEGION GUARDS stand by the doorway.

LURAS halts.

                            LURAS
                Sera.

He looks around.

                            LURAS (CONT'D)
                Sera!

SERA hurries closer.

                            SERA
                I'm here.

                          LURAS
                You have to get chipped.

LURAS puts a hand on her shoulder.

                          LURAS (CONT'D)
                It's the right thing to do.
                You're getting older. Soon
                you'll need it for everything.
                Identification, work, travel,
                food access.

His fingers tighten slightly.

                          LURAS (CONT'D)
                We can't shelter you like this
                anymore.

SERA lowers her head.

Not for long.


Then she steps ahead of him toward the clinic.

Bright light bleeds a path through the doorway.

INT. CLINIC — RECEPTION — CONTINUOUS

A WOMAN behind the glass writes without looking up.

                         WOMAN
               Any appointments?

                         SERA
               I'm here for a chip.

                         WOMAN
               A chip?

The woman finally lifts her head.

                         WOMAN (CONT'D)
               Weren't you chipped as a baby?
               Everyone is.

LURAS steps in beside SERA.

                         LURAS
               Her mother was homeless. Her
               father was probably some
               addict. Probably died. We
               weren't aware she didn't have
               one until recently.

The woman lowers her head again.

                         WOMAN
               I see.

Her pen moves across the form.

                         WOMAN (CONT'D)
               Name?

                         SERA
               Sera.

Her legs begin to tremble.

                         WOMAN
               I'll call you in a second.


               Waiting room is to the left.

LURAS turns toward the chairs.

                         LURAS
               Go. I'll be here when you're
               done.

SERA walks toward the waiting room.

Her name sounds from the speaker before she reaches a seat.

                         SPEAKER
               Sera. Room two.

Her steps shorten.

                         SPEAKER (CONT'D)
               Sera. Room two.

INT. CLINIC — ROOM TWO — CONTINUOUS

SERA stops before the door.

                         DOCTOR (O.S.)
               Come in. Sit right there,
               please.

The DOCTOR stands and opens a cabinet, taking out a small
machine.

                         DOCTOR
               Don't worry. This won't hurt. I
               just need a small amount of
               blood so we can enter your
               biometrics into the system,
               okay?

SERA shakes harder.

A prick.

A drop.

                         DOCTOR
               See? All done.

The DOCTOR releases the blood into the machine.


The screen blinks orange.

ON SCREEN: MATCH FOUND.

His expression changes.

                            DOCTOR
                  Hm. Strange. One moment.

He leaves too quickly.

SERA stands.

She looks at the machine.

ON SCREEN: MATCH FOUND.

Still blinking.

She moves to the door and looks through the glass.

The DOCTOR is speaking to the guards.

Pointing at the room.

Her shaking stops.

One breath.

Then the door handle moves.

SERA throws herself backward.

The door slams into the first guard.

                             GUARD
                  Get her!

She runs.

INT. CLINIC — RECEPTION — CONTINUOUS

The guards raise their weapons.

As SERA passes the waiting room, LURAS stands.

For one second, his eyes find hers.

Then he steps into the line.


Three shots crack through the clinic.

SERA looks back once.

LURAS is already falling.

Then she faces forward and disappears into the night.

                                         FADE TO BLACK.

                            END ACT 09
```
