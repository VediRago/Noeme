```text
      NOEME
A BLURRED TRAGEDY
   written by
 Diogo Oliveira


EXT. CITY STREETS — LUXURY DISTRICT — DAY

The walls separating the city from the luxury apartments no
longer protect them from what the chaos has become.

Cars are parked in the middle of the road, alongside
collapsed bodies, while everyone tries to figure out which
direction to run.

PHIL'S cellphone hits the pavement.

One hand digs inside his pocket while the other widens it,
as if that would help.

A couple pushes through the doors from inside, knocking
PHIL back as they rush past without looking up. One of her
bags drops to the floor.

She keeps going.

INT. PHIL'S APARTMENT BUILDING — LOBBY — CONTINUOUS

The elevator is stuck, neighbors loading everything they
can into it.

Trying to reach his floor, PHIL keeps bumping his glasses
as he presses the keys against his face, rushing to get
home.

When PHIL lowers his head to fit the key into the lock, a
knock from his foot slides the door open.

PHIL pushes it against the wall.

                         PHIL
               ABBY!

A glance toward the kitchen, then down the hall.

                         PHIL (CONT'D)
               ABBY! Come on—we gotta move now!

INT. PHIL'S APARTMENT — STAIRS / HALLWAY — CONTINUOUS

PHIL turns right toward the stairs, only to fumble on the
first step. His hands catch the stairs before his feet do.

PHIL reaches the top with his shoulder running against the
wall.


                            SERA (O.S.)
                 Daddy!

PHIL straightens his glasses in a fast stride toward the
room.

Using his elbow to open the door—

                           PHIL
                 Sera! Where is Mommy?

INT. PHIL'S APARTMENT — SERA'S ROOM — CONTINUOUS

                            SERA
                 Mommyyy!

SERA keeps waving the plushy until it hits him.

A folded piece of paper slips out.

PHIL sees it as it hits the carpet.

SERA'S voice goes muffled.

                            SERA
                 D…y!

PHIL reaches for it.

For a moment, there are two.

Then one.

His hand closes around the paper.

His legs give out.

PHIL misses the bed and hits the floor.

                            SERA
                 …y!

The plushy lands against his foot.

PHIL looks up.

Nothing reaches him.

And he opens it.


Smudged letters gradually shape into words.

ON THE LETTER

"Phil…"

His breathing destabilizes the letter.

                         SERA (O.S.)
                …y!

ON THE LETTER

"I don’t have to tell you how hard this is. It’s
impossible, and you know…"

His head falls back as the letter touches his chest.

His hands start to crumple it as a stream of tears guides
him downward toward what he must face.

As PHIL turns it over, tears fall over the faded words his
wife wrote.

ON THE LETTER

"It’s been what? 23 y eAR s"

"I jus y u ay 27…"

"Every second of every day, you know…"

"I’m counting on you, Phil. Save her, Phil. We knew this
would happen."

"We saved tHo se woR Ds for tHi_s m      ent."

"Here they are…"

"I        ou"

PHIL crumples the letter, takes a deep breath, and removes
his glasses to wipe the tears.

PHIL closes his eyes and breathes in again.

Commotion comes through the windows. People screaming.

PHIL regains his composure, jumping a few times before
slamming his fist into the wall.


Then PHIL looks back at his daughter.

SERA only looks at him.

No crying.

No words.

                         PHIL
               Baby girl, we have to go.

PHIL walks to the window and back.

                          PHIL (CONT'D)
               Ok… Ok…

                          PHIL (CONT'D)
               Food.

PHIL runs toward the kitchen, opens the fridge, looks
through the pantry, and when he faces the table, everything
is already prepared.

A heart saying, “thank you… you know…”

PHIL starts to feel dizzy again. His hand lands on the
table before another stream of tears takes over him.

                          PHIL
               Abby…

                          PHIL (CONT'D)
               Abby.

                          PHIL (CONT'D)
               ABBY!

PHIL picks up a chair and throws it against the wall,
kicking the leg of the table right after.

Looking at the ceiling.

                          PHIL
               I can…

                         PHIL (CONT'D)
               I can do this…

                          PHIL (CONT'D)


               I can’t…

                          PHIL (CONT'D)
               Why…

PHIL grabs the back of his head, pulling his hair.

                          PHIL
               Sera!

                         PHIL (CONT'D)
               Baby, I’m coming.

PHIL picks up the prepared meals and goes toward the room.

PHIL stops by SERA'S room to pick up some clothes and
shoves them into her backpack.

                         PHIL
               Sera! We have to go, baby. Come
               on.

When PHIL walks into the room, SERA is already putting her
crayons aside.

                         PHIL
               Sera, Mommy…

PHIL turns his head away from her and removes his glasses
again to wipe his eyes.

                         PHIL (CONT'D)
               Mommy will be away for a while,
               okay?

                          SERA
               Mommy!

                         PHIL
               I know, baby.

PHIL forces the glasses back on.

                         PHIL (CONT'D)
               Come on. Daddy will take you
               piggyback, okay?

                          SERA
               Mommy!


                         PHIL
               Come on, sweetie. Let’s go.

PHIL pulls the backpack into one hand and lifts SERA onto
his back.

SERA'S arms close around his neck.

EXT. CITY STREETS — LUXURY DISTRICT — CONTINUOUS

They rush toward the street-glow.

Cars block the exit from the walled section. People keep
trying anyway, engines screaming, bumpers pushing into
bumpers as if metal could make a way through metal.

PHIL turns toward the garage.

A car slams into another near the entrance, trying to shove
it aside.

                         PHIL
               Baby, close your eyes, okay?

SERA presses her face against his shoulder.

PHIL reaches the garage and stops.

No exit.

Cars packed across every lane. Doors left open. Alarms
crying into one another.

His hand goes to his pocket.

Nothing.

His phone is gone.

                          PHIL
               The lab…

PHIL looks down the street.

                         PHIL (CONT'D)
               Sera, I’m going to run. Hold on
               tight.

PHIL runs.


Gunshots crack somewhere far away.

Then screams.

Then the screams change, spreading faster than the shots.

A blast rolls through the district behind him.

SERA tightens around his neck.

PHIL does not stop.

INT. RESEARCH FACILITY — ENTRANCE — CONTINUOUS

By the time PHIL reaches the lab, the doors are half-open
and people are already moving the wrong way.

A COLLEAGUE catches sight of him near the entrance.

                          COLLEAGUE
                Phil? What are you doing here?

                          PHIL
                I have to get inside. I need to
                contact—

                           COLLEAGUE
                They’re coming. You know the
                procedure.

The man puts a hand on his shoulder.

                          COLLEAGUE (CONT'D)
                Be safe. Don’t let them find
                you here.

Another blast hits closer.

PHIL pulls away.

INT. RESEARCH FACILITY — HALLWAY — CONTINUOUS

Inside, the halls are too bright. Too clean. Too empty.

PHIL reaches his office with SERA still on his back and
pushes the door open hard enough to hit the wall.

                          PHIL
                Sera, stay here, okay?


PHIL lowers her to the floor.

Then PHIL starts tearing through the desk.

Chairs scrape. Drawers open. Papers spill. Something glass
breaks against the floor.

                         PHIL
               Fuck. Come on.

A blast shakes the building.

PHIL finds a pen.

Then paper.

His hand moves too fast for the words to look right.

PHIL folds the page once.

Then again.

PHIL crouches in front of SERA and slides it into one of
her pockets.

                         PHIL
               Keep this, okay?

SERA looks at him.

PHIL touches her face.

                         PHIL (CONT'D)
               Baby, come.

INT. RESEARCH FACILITY — DISPLACEMENT CHAMBER — CONTINUOUS

PHIL takes her into the device room.

The chair waits in the center.

PHIL straps her in with shaking hands.

                            SERA
               Daddy?

                         PHIL
               I know. I know.

PHIL turns to the console.


                           GUARD (O.S.)
               STOP!

Two armed guards enter with their weapons raised.

                         GUARD
               Step away from the machine!

PHIL keeps typing.

                         GUARD (CONT'D)
               Stop right now or we shoot!

His fingers move faster.

                         GUARD (CONT'D)
               Who are you? What are you doing
               here?

PHIL'S hand stops over the keyboard.

                           PHIL
               Who am I?

PHIL turns slowly.

                           PHIL (CONT'D)
               Who am I?

The guards keep their weapons on him.

                         PHIL (CONT'D)
               This exists because of me.

SERA starts crying.

                         PHIL (CONT'D)
               All of this. This room. This
               machine. These years.

                           PHIL (CONT'D)
               My wife.

A breath.

                         PHIL (CONT'D)
               My daughter.

The guards hesitate.


                            GUARD 1
               Sir…

One of them lowers his weapon slightly.

                         GUARD 1 (CONT'D)
               Calm down. We understand. Your
               daughter—

                         PHIL
               My daughter?

PHIL laughs once.

His hand drags up his face and stops against his forehead
as his head tilts back.

                         PHIL (CONT'D)
               I lost my wife and my daughter
               to this piece of crap.

His knees weaken.

PHIL catches himself against the console.

                            PHIL (CONT'D)
               Abby…

A breath catches.

                            PHIL (CONT'D)
               I won’t—

The third soldier enters.

                         THIRD SOLDIER
               What’s going on here?

A shot cuts through the room.

PHIL'S body folds forward.

His hand hits the key before he falls.

                                            FADE TO BLACK.

                            END ACT 05
```
