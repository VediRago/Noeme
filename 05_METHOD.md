# Method

NOEME is built through layered documents that look at the same story from different distances.

## The Pipeline

### Act Guide

Defines the act’s purpose, atmosphere, emotional movement, visual language, and major motifs.

### Blueprint

Breaks scenes into direction, visual attention, reveal structure, pacing, and emotional progression.

### Prose

Presents the story in its most readable form, with atmosphere, emotion, rhythm, and sensory experience at the front.

### Screenplay

Translates the material into production-facing format through image, action, dialogue, timing, and execution.

## Layer Purpose

**Act Guide:** What am I about to experience?

**Blueprint:** How does the scene work?

**Prose:** What does the story feel like when read?

**Screenplay:** How is the story communicated for production?
