# NOEME — Pitch


NOEME is a story of contradiction: how can everything be connected if it only exists through separation? An inevitable path paved with tragedy, hope, love, and guilt, where lives shape one another without ever understanding the cost — least of all herself.

Within a system that enforces separation, the self fractures into separate lives, each carrying a different outcomes. Lucreta opens the door to Phil and Abby, Sera's parents, before Sera is even born. Her red hair and green eyes echo Lucreta's, forming the first visible step toward a vast spectrum of inherited feeling — ending in a tragedy that breaks the very system that caused this self to end young.

NOEME follows these separate lives as human stories first. A bride collapses before reaching her wedding. A newly married couple endures a suffocating city.. A child not yet born seems to be the answer. 

The series is built around recognition. The audience should feel the connection before they can define it.
