# About

I'm Diogo Oliveira.

NOEME started from a single idea: the need to find reason inside things that seem disconnected.

That way of thinking has always shaped how I look at life. I question patterns, causes, contradictions, and the hidden links between people and events. NOEME became the story where those questions could take shape.

While developing it, I began documenting the process around the story itself: the writing, worldbuilding, scene design, structure, visual prototyping, and the methods that started appearing as patterns.

This portfolio brings that work together. It shows NOEME as both a creative project and a design process.

My goal is to keep developing these skills and find work that lets me grow further in narrative design, storytelling, and worldbuilding.
