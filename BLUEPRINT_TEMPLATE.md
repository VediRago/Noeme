# Scene Blueprint Template

A scene blueprint is for one specific scene.

It should keep all production-useful scene information, but avoid repeating information already carried by the Act Guide.

## Core Rule

Act Guide = whole-act information.  
Scene Blueprint = scene-specific information.

Do not remove useful scene detail.

Remove or reduce only the material that explains the whole act, the whole world, or the whole motif system.
