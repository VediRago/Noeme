## Act Feeling

Parents begins with the shape of a wedding that actually opens into life.

The chapel is cheap and artificial, but Philip and Abigail are not.

The act should feel alive, pressured, funny, crowded, and increasingly open.

The city presses around them with noise, bodies, fog, traffic, and poverty.

Phil and Abby survive it through rhythm.

Their banter is not filler. It is how they keep moving together.

The lake should feel like the first real breath of the act.

## Act Purpose

Act 02 introduces Philip and Abigail through movement, pressure, humor, and emotional contrast.

It answers Lucreta's failed threshold with a living one.

Lucreta collapses before crossing the threshold of her wedding.

Philip and Abigail begin by crossing theirs.

Part 01 moves them from a fake chapel celebration, through the pressure of the city, toward distance, silence, vastness, and the reveal of their new home.

## Setting & Atmosphere

Parents begins in a futuristic compressed city.

The city should feel crowded, loud, fogged, indifferent, and too full of itself to leave room for sky.

The car is temporary shelter, not true escape.

The road toward the lake should gradually open the world.

The final reveal should feel intimate, not grandiose.

## Important Motifs

**Doorway**  
The chapel doorway opens the act into life, directly contrasting Lucreta's failed wedding threshold.

**City Pressure**  
The city is not a single villain. It is an environment of compression, noise, poverty, fog, and interruption.

**Banter**  
Phil and Abby's humor is their survival language. It keeps warmth alive inside pressure.

**Window / Glass**  
The car window separates Abby from the city and lets her drift into reflection.

**Sky / Vastness**  
The missing sky defines the city. The lake restores space, distance, and breath.

**Key / Home**  
The key turns the lake from scenery into future. Part 01 ends on: "This is our new home."

Gives the act breathing room as the city thins and time passes.

### 90 — First Sight Of The Lake

Lets Abby experience vastness before the key is revealed.

### 100 — Key Reveal / New Home

Turns the lake into future as Phil reveals the key and tells Abby this is their new home.
